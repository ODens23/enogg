All About ENOGG LIMITED!

Know more about Enogg Limited


ENOGG is an indigenous EPCOM (Engineering, Procurement, Construction, Operation and Maintenance); company with unsurpassed experience in civil, consultancy, mechanical and power sector of Nigeria economy.
Our team of professional and technical personnel are seasoned Engineers who are duly certfied by COREN and NEMSA. We execute excellent performance in effective job delivery has earned ENOGG several recognition in consultancy and power sector of the economy both at the national and international level. In our quest for best practices and optimal performance ENOGG established technical partnership with ABB Houston Texas, Siemens, Schneider Electric, OTDS UK etc.

KEY BACKGROUND
1. ENOGG Engineering Nigeria Limited is an indigenous electrical engineering and construction company with vast experience and track record of completed projects particularly in Edo State.
2. ENOGG was incorporated by the Corporate Affairs Commission, Abuja on April 26th, 2013.
3. The Company Management is led by executives with decases of experience in the Power Sector.
4. The Company is supported by an Advisory Board comprising long-standing senior executives from the NESI (Nigeiran Electricity Supply Industry) and the International Energy Industry.
5. The Company management is supported by a strong and dedicated team of engineers and technicians which comprises of indigenous engineers and expatriates.
6. The Company HSE policies are designed to meet the highest national and international standards with a dedicated HSE Team.
7. The Company maintains required certification relevant to design, engineering, procurement, commissioning, operation & maintenance in the Oil and Gas Industry and NESI.
